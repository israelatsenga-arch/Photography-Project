About

These are a few photos from my favourite animated movie,'Spider-man Across the Spider-Verse'
These are some of the characters who stood out for me with their biographies included.

Miles Morales

He is a superhero from Brooklyn,Newyork.
He got his powers from a radioactive spider from earth-42 and chose to use his powers to protect people.

The Prowler

He is a supervillan from another universe.
He is actually Miles Morales of earth-42 which is the best plot twist.

Gwen Stacy

Also known as Ghost spider.
She is an alternate-universe version Gwen Stacy from earth-65.
she was bitten by a radio active spider as a teenager and becomes known as Spider-Gwen.

Miguel O'hara

Also known as Spider-man 2099.
He is a superhero from the future who attempts to recreate the abilities of the original spiderman.
He later suffers a related accident that causes half of his DNA to be rewritten with a spider's genetic code.
